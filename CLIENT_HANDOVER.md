# Aayat Real Estate: Client Handover Manual

Welcome to your new digital portfolio. This system has been engineered to provide you with complete control over your website's content without needing any technical assistance.

## Accessing the CMS (Admin Dashboard)

1. Navigate to: `https://yourwebsite.com/admin/login`
2. Enter your secure credentials (provided by your development team).
3. Upon logging in, you will see your Dashboard.

---

## 1. Managing Properties

Your properties are the core of your website.

- **To Add a Property**: Click "Add Property" in the Properties tab. Fill in the wizard step-by-step.
- **Images**: Drag and drop images into the uploader. The system automatically compresses them for fast loading. Drag to reorder them; the first image is always the Cover Image.
- **Featured**: Check the "Featured" box to display a property prominently on the homepage.
- **Views**: The dashboard automatically tracks and displays how many times each property has been viewed.

## 2. Managing the Hero Banner

The Hero Banner is the large visual section at the top of your homepage.

- **Desktop vs. Mobile**: You can upload distinct images/videos for desktop and mobile devices. This ensures your site always looks perfect, regardless of the screen size.
- **To Update**: Go to the "Hero Banners" tab, click "Add Banner" (or edit an existing one), and set it as the **Default**. The homepage will instantly reflect this change.

## 3. Lead Management & Analytics

- **Source Tracking**: When a lead arrives, the "Source" column tells you exactly where they came from (e.g., "Contact: Book Visit", "Property: The Address", or "Popup").
- **Status Pipeline**: Keep your sales process organized by updating lead statuses from "New" to "Contacted", "Qualified", or "Closed".
- **Follow-up**: Click a client's phone number or email directly in the table to instantly initiate a call or draft an email.

## 4. Site Content & SEO

In the **Site Content** tab, you can manage global settings:
- Update your company phone number, WhatsApp link, email, and address.
- Change the homepage "About" section and the Footer text.
- Connect Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or Microsoft Clarity simply by pasting your tracking IDs. No coding required.

## Security Notice
- Always log out when accessing the dashboard from a shared computer.
- Do not share your password via insecure channels.
